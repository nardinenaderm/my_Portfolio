About Me

Hello! My name is Nardine Nader, I graduated in 2024 with a dual degree in Computer Science from Ain Shams University and the University of East London, majoring in Multimedia and Computer Graphics. For my graduation project, I developed a Virtual Reality Chemistry Lab, combining interactive design with immersive technology.

After graduation, I joined the CG Technical Director Track at the Information Technology Institute (ITI), where I gained first-hand experience in a full movie production pipeline. During this time, I also created my first graphics engine using the Vulkan API, strengthening my technical foundation in computer graphics.

I am proficient in Houdini, Autodesk Maya, Unreal Engine, and Unity, with additional knowledge of Nuke.
